Bubble-blowing contest

A bubble-blowing contest can be a lot of fun for people of all ages! Here's a breakdown of how to play, with some variations:

Basic Concepts:

Objective:

The core idea is to see who can blow the biggest, longest-lasting, or most impressive bubbles.

Materials:

Bubble solution (store-bought or homemade).
Bubble wands (various sizes and shapes add to the fun).
Optional: Measuring tools (ruler, tape measure).

Contest Variations:

Biggest Bubble
Longest-Lasting Bubble
Most Bubbles
Bubble Shapes/Artistry
Bubble Relay/Race
