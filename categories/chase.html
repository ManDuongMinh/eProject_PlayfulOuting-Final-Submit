Chasing

A chasing game is a game where one or more players try to tag other players. Chasing games are also known as tag games

How to play:

Choose a "chaser":

The chaser tries to tag the other players

Tagged-player:

The tagged players become "in"

Game end:

The game ends when there's only one player left who hasn't been tagged

Benifit:

Chasing games help develop skills like reading the opposition They can also help improve aerobic capacity, strength, endurance, and flexibility
