Ring the toss

To play "ring toss," players take turns throwing rings onto a target with hooks or pegs, aiming to land the rings around the designated spots on the target; the goal is usually to be the first to reach a set number of points, with each successful ring landing on a peg earning a specific point value depending on its location on the target

Key points about playing ring toss:

Set up:

Place the target (with hooks or pegs) at a suitable distance from the throwing line based on player skill level.

Throwing rings:

Each player takes turns throwing their rings, attempting to land them around the hooks or pegs on the target.

Scoring:

Different pegs may have different point values, with rings landing on more difficult spots earning higher points. .

Winning:

The first player to reach a predetermined number of points wins the game.
