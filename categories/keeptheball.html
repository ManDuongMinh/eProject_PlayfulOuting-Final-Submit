Keep the ball in the air

>"Keep the ball in the air" is a game where players work together to keep a ball or balloon in the air as long as possible. It can be played in a variety of ways, including with groups, pairs, or individuals.

How to play

1. Choose a ball or balloon
2. Divide players into groups
3. Have one player hit the ball into the air
4. Players take turns hitting or tossing the ball to each other
5. Players work together to keep the ball in the air
6. Count how many times the ball is hit before it hits the ground
7. Try to improve your score each round

Variations:

Use different types of balls, such as foam balls, beach balls, or volleyballs Add more balls to the game Play in pairs or groups
