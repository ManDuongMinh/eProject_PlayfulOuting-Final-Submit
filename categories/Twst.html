Twister

Twister is a classic party game that involves flexibility, coordination, and a lot of laughs.

Here's how to play:

Ensure there's enough space around the mat for players to move.

The Spinner:

Place the spinner within easy reach of the designated spinner (or referee).

Gameplay

The Spinner's Role:

One person acts as the spinner (or referee). They spin the dial. The spinner calls out the body part and color that the dial lands on (e.g., "Right hand, red!").

Player's Actions:

All players must then attempt to place the called-out body part on a vacant circle of the called-out color.

Elimination:

A player is eliminated if: They fall or touch the mat with a knee or elbow. They can't reach a required circle. They lift a hand or foot from its circle, without announcing it to allow another limb to pass.
