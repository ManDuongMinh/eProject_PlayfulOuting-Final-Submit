Hoi An – The Ancient Town of Vibrant Colors

Hoi An is an ancient city, famous for its shimmering lantern-lit streets, unique architecture, and appealing cuisine.

Featured Attractions:

Hoi An Ancient Town:

Stroll along the small streets, admire the ancient houses and the brilliant lanterns.

Japanese Covered Bridge:

Admire the symbol of Hoi An, a curved tile-roofed wooden bridge spanning the Hoai River.

Tan Ky Old House:

Explore one of the best-preserved ancient houses in Hoi An, with traditional architecture.

Exciting Activities:

Boat Ride on the Hoai River:

View the ancient town from a different perspective, especially at night when the lanterns shimmer.

Visit Tailoring Shops:

Tailor unique outfits in Hoi An style.

Enjoy Local Cuisine:

Discover specialty dishes like cao lau, mi quang, white rose dumplings...
