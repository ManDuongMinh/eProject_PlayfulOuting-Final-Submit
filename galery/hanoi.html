Hanoi – The Thousand-Year-Old Capital of Culture

Hanoi is the capital of Vietnam, famous for its ancient architectural works, distinctive culinary culture, and peaceful life.

Featured Attractions:

Hoan Kiem Lake:

The symbol of Hanoi, associated with the legend of King Le Loi returning the sword to the Golden Turtle.

Temple of Literature – Imperial Academy:

Vietnam's first university, preserving traditional cultural and educational values.

Hanoi Old Quarter:

An old quarter with narrow streets, ancient houses, and specialty dishes.

Exciting Activities:

Enjoy Egg Coffee:

A distinctive Hanoi beverage, loved by many.

Watch Water Puppet Shows:

A unique traditional art form of Vietnam.

Stroll Around West Lake:

Enjoy the open space and admire the beautiful scenery of the lake.
