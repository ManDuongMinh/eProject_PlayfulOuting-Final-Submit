Northwest Vietnam – The Majestic Beauty of Mountains and Forests

Northwest Vietnam is a majestic land with towering mountain ranges, picturesque terraced fields, and unique ethnic villages.

Featured Attractions:

Sapa:

A misty town famous for Fansipan, the "Roof of Indochina".

Mu Cang Chai:

Home to Vietnam's most beautiful terraced fields, especially during the rice harvest season.

Dien Bien Phu:

An important historical site, where the glorious Dien Bien Phu victory took place.

Exciting Activities:

Trekking and Mountain Climbing:

Explore the majestic beauty of the Northwest mountains and forests through trekking and climbing trails.

Visit Ethnic Villages:

Learn about the culture and customs of ethnic minorities such as H'mong, Thai, Dao...

Enjoy Local Cuisine:

Discover specialty dishes of the Northwest mountains such as thang co, five-color sticky rice, kitchen-smoked buffalo meat...
