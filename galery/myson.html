My Son Sanctuary – Cham Pa Cultural Heritage

My Son Sanctuary is an ancient Cham Pa temple tower architectural complex, recognized by UNESCO as a World Cultural Heritage Site.

Featured Attractions:

Ancient Temple Towers:

Explore unique architectural works, bearing the cultural imprint of the Cham Pa.

My Son Museum:

Learn about Cham Pa history and culture through the exhibited artifacts.

Art Performance Programs:

Enjoy traditional Cham Pa dance performances.

Sightseeing and Photography:
