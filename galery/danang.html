Da Nang – A Livable City

Da Nang is a beautiful coastal city, famous for its unique bridges, the charming My Khe beach, and diverse cuisine.

Featured Attractions:

Dragon Bridge:

The symbol of Da Nang, capable of spraying fire and water on weekends.

Ba Na Hills:

A famous eco-tourism area with the Golden Bridge and the ancient French Village.

My Khe Beach:

One of the most beautiful beaches on the planet, with fine white sand and clear blue water.

Exciting Activities:

Swimming and Snorkeling:

Explore the beauty of the ocean at the beaches surrounding the city.

Enjoy Fresh Seafood:

Discover local cuisine with fresh and delicious seafood dishes.

Visit the Cham Museum:

Learn about the history and culture of the Champa through the exhibited artifacts.
