Ho Chi Minh City – The Pearl of the Far East

Ho Chi Minh City is the largest economic and cultural center of Vietnam, famous for its unique architectural works, diverse cuisine, and vibrant life.

Featured Attractions:

Notre Dame Cathedral:

A classic French architectural work, the symbol of the city.

Central Post Office:

One of the most beautiful French architectural works in Vietnam.

Independence Palace:

An important historical relic, where many significant events of the country took place.

Exciting Activities:

Explore Street Food:

Enjoy specialty dishes such as banh mi, pho, hu tieu...

Shopping at Ben Thanh Market:

Experience the bustling atmosphere of buying and selling and find unique souvenirs.

Visit Museums:

Learn about the history, culture, and art of the city.
