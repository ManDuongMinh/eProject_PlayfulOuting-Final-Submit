Chợ nổi Cái Răng – Cai Rang Floating Market

Chợ nổi Cái Răng (Cai Rang Floating Market) is a wholesale market specializing in fruits and agricultural products of the Mekong Delta region, located in Cai Rang district, Can Tho city. It is one of the largest and most famous floating markets in the Mekong Delta. The market operates very early, usually from 3 am to 9 am.

Wholesale Trade:

The market primarily functions as a wholesale hub, attracting traders from across the Mekong Delta and beyond.
The main goods traded are fresh produce, including a wide variety of fruits (mangoes, pineapples, oranges, etc.), vegetables, and other agricultural products.

Bartering and Exchange:

The market is a spectacle of countless boats, large and small, creating a lively and energetic atmosphere.
Vendors use a tall pole called a "cây bẹo" to display samples of their goods, making it easy for buyers to identify what's for sale.
Transactions happen directly on the boats, showcasing a unique aspect of the region's river culture.

Food and Services:

Alongside trade, the market features boats offering breakfast options like "hủ tiếu" (noodle soup), "bún riêu" (crab noodle soup), and coffee.
Visitors can hire boats to explore the market, experience the local way of life, and savor regional specialties.

Tourism:

Chợ nổi Cái Răng is a popular tourist destination, drawing both domestic and international visitors.
Tourists come to witness the unique floating market culture, enjoy the scenic river views, and purchase local products.
